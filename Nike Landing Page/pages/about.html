About Nike

Innovation, Performance, and Inspiration. This is Nike.

Our Story

Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, Nike has evolved into the world's leading athletic brand. With a relentless pursuit of innovation, we create products that inspire and empower athletes at all levels.

From the iconic Air Jordans to groundbreaking running shoes, Nike has shaped the sportswear industry by blending performance and culture.

Our Mission

To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. (*If you have a body, you are an athlete.)

Our Values

Innovation

We push the limits of design and technology to create the best products for athletes worldwide.

Sustainability

We are committed to a greener future through responsible manufacturing and recycled materials.

Performance

Our gear is designed to enhance performance, comfort, and style, helping athletes reach their potential.

Our Legacy

For over five decades, Nike has shaped the future of sports and culture. From groundbreaking advertisements to sponsoring the world's greatest athletes, we continue to push the boundaries of excellence.

We revolutionized sneaker culture with the Air Max series, set new records with the Vaporfly, and redefined basketball shoes with Air Jordans.

Innovation & Technology

At Nike, we are obsessed with pushing boundaries. Our research labs work with top athletes to develop revolutionary technology like Flyknit, ZoomX, and React Foam.

From self-lacing shoes to data-driven performance wear, our innovations are designed to enhance movement, speed, and comfort.

Sustainability & Responsibility

We believe in a zero-carbon, zero-waste future. Our Move to Zero initiative is focused on reducing our environmental impact through recycled materials and sustainable production.

By 2025, we aim to use 100% renewable energy across our global operations. We also work with communities to ensure fair labor practices and positive social impact.

Athletes Who Inspire Us
